7 WONDERS OF THE WORLD

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • Flavorings (e.g., chocolate chips, fruits, nuts) as desired

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and sugar until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add the whole milk and vanilla extract. Whisk until well combined.
  3. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  4. If desired, add flavorings such as chocolate chips, fruits, or nuts during the last few minutes of churning.
  5. Transfer the churned ice cream to a lidded container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.
  6. Scoop and serve the delicious homemade ice cream.

Vegetable Biryani

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/4cup cashews
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pan, heat ghee and add cumin seeds. Sauté until they splutter.
  3. Add sliced onions, minced garlic, grated ginger, and sliced green chilies. Sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  4. Add diced carrots, potatoes, and green peas. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pan. Stir well.
  6. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix everything together.
  7. Add 2 cups of water and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover the pan, and let it simmer
    for about 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the flavors are well blended.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and cashews. Serve hot.
